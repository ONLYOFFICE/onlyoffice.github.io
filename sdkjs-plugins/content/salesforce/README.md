## Overview

Import data from Salesforce into your spreadsheet and export records back to Salesforce without leaving ONLYOFFICE.

The plugin lets you connect to your Salesforce org, browse standard and custom objects, query reports, and pull the results directly into spreadsheet cells as a formatted table. You can also select rows in the spreadsheet and push them back to Salesforce as new or updated records.

The Salesforce plugin is compatible with [self-hosted](https://github.com/ONLYOFFICE/DocumentServer) and [desktop](https://github.com/ONLYOFFICE/DesktopEditors) versions of ONLYOFFICE editors.

## How to use

1. Open a spreadsheet and go to the **Plugins** tab, then click **Salesforce**.

2. Click **Log in** and authorize the plugin with your Salesforce account via OAuth.

3. Choose an import source:
   - **Objects** — browse standard and custom Salesforce objects, select fields, and apply filters.
   - **Reports** — pick an existing Salesforce report to import its result set.

4. Click **Import** to paste the data into your spreadsheet starting from the active cell.

5. To export data back to Salesforce, select the rows in your spreadsheet and use the **Export** option to create or update records in the chosen object.
